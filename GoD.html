Gears of Dragoon: Fragments of a New Era

Dungeon-Crawler/Visual Novel
Company: JAST
Official Site


Conflict simmers in the city of Carnas. The magic-wielding ruling elite and the technologically-inclined underclass coexist in an uneasy equilibrium. It’s only a matter of time before that balance is disturbed, though, for beneath Carnas lurks the Dragonschaft, a sprawling, seemingly bottomless labyrinth full of perils and treasures alike. A myriad of secrets await those brave or foolish enough to prowl its twisting passageways—secrets that could change the world forever… or even end it.
