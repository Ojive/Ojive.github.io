Genso Manège

Otome Visual Novel
Company: PQube
Official Site


A beautifully produced festival of romance! Follow Emma as she fights to reawaken her magical powers in order to aid a unique cast of characters, all of whom are trapped inside a magical travelling amusement park. Having lost her memories in an accident as a child, Emma must rediscover her past through love and friendship in this stunning dream-like fantasy Otome.

Releases sometime in 2024!
