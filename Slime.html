Volume 15
I've been killing slimes for 300 years, and my family has finally decided on my birthday! We didn’t have a date until now (mainly because I forgot it…), but my daughters are going to celebrate it with me this year, and I'm really, really excited! Later, I meet the goddess of fate (I can't be surprised anymore!), I help out a phantom thief (and my family grows), and I visit a strange land where there are no slimes! I'll share another short story from a drama CD at the end, so don't miss it!

Volume 16
I've been killing slimes for 300 years, and now my family is trying for comedy gold?! I was just engaging in some light banter about being reincarnated when my daughter recognized my talent (?) for snappy comebacks and asked me to write a script… Meanwhile, our new family member, the mimic, warms up to Halkara (they go on a walk together) and I try my hand at the gods' newest video game (or should I say newest world?). And finally, Laika's back at it in The Red-Dragon Academy for Girls!
